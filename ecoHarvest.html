EcoHarvest

Project Overview

EcoHarvest is an innovative project focused on sustainable agriculture and environmental impact reduction. It uses smart farming techniques and IoT-based sensors to optimize water use and crop growth while minimizing waste. The project aims to empower local farmers with technology that improves yield and supports eco-friendly practices.

Features

  • Smart sensor networks to monitor soil and crop health
  • Machine learning for predictive crop analysis
  • Mobile app for real-time farm monitoring and alerts
  • Community engagement for sustainable farming education
